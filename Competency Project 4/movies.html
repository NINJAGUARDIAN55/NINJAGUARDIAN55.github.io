----- The Shards Movie Series -----

The Shard series is about a boy who recieves the power of magnetism after the original possessor dies saving his life. The Boy is telported through space on a dying wish to save the planet of the man who saved him. He arrives to a state of chaos and battle where he will find his true self and who he can become. In this epic journey, friends unite, evil arises, will our hero triumph? Only you can know!






Shards: The Beginnging

The Journey begins as Jake recieves his power and is sent to save the planet of man who saved his life. He must learn how to use his power and unite the 3 divided clans inorder to defeat a growing evil.



Shards: Revolution

A small victory. The alliance has been formed! However, the alliance is still weak and in need of greater aid in deafeating the threat of the Red Ants. There has been talk of freeing captives, but will it be done in time?



Shards: The Void

A great loss for a small victory. With heavy casualties from rescuing the captives, Jason disheartened, heads off on his own to deal with his guilt. Discovering a new and catastrophic weapon of his enemie, he searches for his friends. Will the weapon be stopped in time?



Shards: Anihilation

n the edge of destruction, Jason and his friends have one last chance to defeat the Red Ants. But their running out of time. In the greatest interplanet battle to be fought, our hero will prove what he is really made of. This is the final written work of The World of Shards.
